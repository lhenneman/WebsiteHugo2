About this Data

Scenarios

Baseline: The base case absolute concentrations from the CMAQ model.

Small cordon with no toll on 395 A small cordon area in downtown DC.
Small cordon with toll on 395 The small cordon plus major through-routes.
Large cordon A larger pricing cordon covering most of DC.

Tolls: Low Toll ( $ ) vs High Toll ( $$ ).

Pollutants

NO2: Best pollutant indicator of traffic pollution from tailpipes. Harmful gas.

O3 (Ozone): Harmful gas that requires other pollutants + sunlight to form (more complex relationships vs. other pollutants).

PM2.5: Small particles that are associated with mortality and other adverse health outcomes. Generated by traffic (e.g., brake and tire wear) and formed from emitted NO2.
